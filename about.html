About Me

I am currently a student at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor pursuing a degree in Computer Science Engineering. I have a wide range of interests from Web Development to Human Computer Interactions to Machine Learning. The brunt of my coursework has been in subjects that emphasize algorithms, logic, and concepts in mathematics.
